Meet the Nerds

Hosts

Dr. Pants

Dr. Pants is the resident robotics fanatic of the Nerdstrodamus crew — if it’s got gears, circuits, or a shiny metal chassis, he’s already in love. Whether he’s building them, battling them, or just talking your ear off about why robots are the future, his passion is unmatched. In-game, expect him to gravitate toward anything mechanical… and probably name it.

Lambo

Lambo is the lovable wildcard of the Nerdstrodamus crew — part chaos engine, part mystery. Nobody’s quite sure if his wild plays are strokes of genius or happy accidents, and he’s not telling. He’s known for clutch saves, questionable strategies, and jokes that hit harder than his headshots. Whatever the game, if Lambo’s in the lobby, something unforgettable is about to happen.

Correspondents

Punk

Punk is one of Nerdstrodamus’ correspondents and the host of What the Game!, the channel’s anything-goes game show. He mainly games on PC but always keeps his Steam Deck and Switch 2 close for those away-from-the-desk sessions. He’s all about games you can play with friends, but he’ll never say no to a solid single-player story. Expect good vibes, a bit of chaos, and plenty of playful trash talk.

Slotad

Slotad is the steady hand of the Nerdstrodamus crew — until he’s not. Known for his sharp instincts, dry humor, and occasional streaks of absolute chaos, he’s equally at home pulling off a perfectly timed play or derailing the plan entirely. Whether he’s in it for the win or just for the laughs, Slotad always keeps things interesting.

DAAD

DAAD is the unpredictable spark of the Nerdstrodamus crew — always ready to stir things up and keep everyone on their toes. When he’s not bringing chaos to the game, he’s perfecting the art of smoking meat, because nothing pairs with a good match like a slow-cooked masterpiece. Clever plays, offbeat humor, and a little unpredictability make every game with DAAD an adventure.
